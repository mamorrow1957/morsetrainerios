Privacy Policy

Morse Trainer  |  Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

Morse Trainer is a free app that helps you learn and practice Morse code. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app does — and does not — collect.

Data Collection

Morse Trainer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed.
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party tracking libraries are included.

Network Requests

The app fetches random article summaries from the public Wikipedia REST API solely to provide practice content. No personal data is sent in these requests. Wikipedia's own privacy policy governs their handling of API traffic.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
